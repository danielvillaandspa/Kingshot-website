About the Game

What Is KingShot?

KingShot is an online mobile strategy and simulation game developed by Century Games. Players take on the role of Governors who must battle rebels, rescue refugees, and rekindle civilization in a dark era of collapse.

Starting from a small Town Center, you develop your city, provide shelter and food to civilians, train soldiers, recruit heroes, and gather resources to grow your power. As your kingdom expands, you compete against governors worldwide to claim the ultimate throne.

The continent is flooded with untapped resources following a sudden state collapse. Refugees, rebels, and power-hungry governors all eye these precious materials — only the strongest and most strategic Governor will prevail.

Core Gameplay

How You Play

Multiple paths to power — choose your strategy and dominate the realm.

⚒️ City Development
Develop your Town Center, rescue civilians, provide food and shelter, and plan city constructions. A strong base is the foundation of any empire.
⚔️ PvP Warfare
Compete against governors worldwide. March your armies to attack, pillage resources, and rise through the power rankings to become the strongest Governor.
🛡️ Alliance Warfare
Fight alongside your alliance in Sanctuary Battles, Kingdom of Power events, and cross-server wars. Claim fortresses and expand your alliance territory.
